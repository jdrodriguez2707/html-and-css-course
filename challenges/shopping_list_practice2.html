Recommended recipe

Recipe with porkü•©

  • Cut a piece of pork loin into cubes and season them with salt and pepper.
  • Fry a chopped onion and a garlic clove in a pot with oil.
  • Add the pork, a bay leaf, a teaspoon of paprika, a glass of white wine and water to cover.
  • Simmer until the pork is tender and the sauce thickens.
  • Serve with rice or potatoes. Enjoy!

About us

We are a company dedicated to facilitating the planning and saving of your purchases in the supermarket. Our mission is to offer you a simple and practical tool so that you can create personalized shopping lists, compare prices, find offers and discounts, and access recipes and ideas for your diet. Our vision is to be the leading platform in the supermarket shopping list market, providing a satisfactory, reliable and secure user experience. We want to help you optimize your time and money, and improve your quality of life. Our values are:

- Innovation: We constantly seek to improve our service, incorporating new features and adapting to the needs of our users.

- Quality: We guarantee a high-quality service, with updated and verified information, and with an intuitive and easy-to-use design.

- Commitment: We are committed to our users, listening to their suggestions and comments, and offering efficient and friendly technical support.

- Responsibility: We are responsible with the environment, promoting responsible and sustainable consumption, and reducing food waste.
